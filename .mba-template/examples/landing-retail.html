Our story

Open since 2017, run by people who actually have pets.

Maya opened Bedford Pet Co. after her rescue lab Bowie struggled to find food that didn't upset his stomach. The shop carries every brand she'd actually feed him — and a few she keeps stocked for the regulars.

We're independent, locally-owned, and pretty much always full of dogs. Bowie usually greets visitors. Bring your pup; we have treats.
